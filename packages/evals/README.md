# @repo/evals

Reusable **evaluation schemas** and **test harness utilities** for AI workflows and agents.

**Status:** scaffold only — align with `benchmarks/` and golden-set practices as they land.

## Important disclaimer

This package holds **engineering shapes** for eval runs and metrics — not a certified evaluation platform, not a substitute for human review of high-risk systems, and **not** legal or compliance attestation. Pair it with your org’s model risk and release processes.
